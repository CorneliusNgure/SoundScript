About Us

Welcome to SoundScript transcription service. SoundScript allows users to upload audio files and convert them to text using AI technology. The project was inspired by the need to make audio-to-text transcription accessible and efficient for everyone. Many of us, including students, journalists, and content creators, often face the tedious task of converting spoken words into written text. Existing solutions are often costly and complex, making the process cumbersome.

I envisioned a tool that simplifies this task by leveraging advanced speech recognition technology. By using AssemblyAI's powerful API, SoundScript aims to deliver high-quality transcriptions through an intuitive interface, saving users valuable time and effort. SoundScript empowers users to focus on what truly matters: capturing important ideas and creating meaningful content without being bogged down by manual transcription. I am passionate about making this technology accessible to all.
